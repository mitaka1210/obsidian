# Journalyst

Plugin ID: `journalyst`
Links: [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Justin-Arnold/Journalyst) or [<button id=HH>Open in Obsidian</button>](obsidian://show-plugin?id=journalyst)
Developed by: [[Justin-Arnold]]
